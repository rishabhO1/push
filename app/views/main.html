Welcome to Push!

This is the first Web project created by me which tries to organise most of the activities you do in the college and keeps you reminded by push notifications.

Class Structure

This feature helps you create a weekly class schedule for yourself and provides you push notifications to keep you reminded of your classes

Attendance

This feature helps you to keep the track of your attendance on the period of your convenience(daily/weekly).

Food Schedule

Health is Wealth! keeping your body well nutritioned is also as important as everything else

Activity Clubs

This feature provides you the information regarding the recent ongoings of the activity clubs of your interest.
